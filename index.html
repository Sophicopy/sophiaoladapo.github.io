WHO IS CELINE TAM?

Celine Tam's Biography

Celine Tam is a Chinese Teenage singer from Hong Kong. She was born on the 20th of July 2009 and her parents are Mr Tam and Mrs Tam. Her parents named her and her little sister "Dion" after the famous secular Canadian artist Celine Dion due to their love for Celine Dion and her songs. She is a singer who has gone places and passed many competitions e.g AGT and the world got talent to become who she is today. She participated in Let's Sing Kids season two and her voice then was incredible despite her young age. She is currently studying in her school and she posts music covers on her youtube channel "celine tam". I will place one of her songs below for you to hear.

She said at the Asian Award Event that she has always loved singing right from her childhood. Her father, Steve Tam who's an expert vocal coach trained her up and helped her fulfil her dream to sing in front of a large audience without fear and for that she remains forever greatful to him.
